Helicity labels are doubled helicities in $¥{-1,+1¥}$. The electron is massless, so $h'=h$ and every amplitude with $h'¥ne h$ vanishes.
¥section*{User-frame kinematics}
Define $¥alpha=¥phi_{¥mathrm{in}}$, $¥varphi=¥phi_{¥mathrm{Out}}$, $w=q_{¥mathrm{Out}}$, and
¥begin{align*}
P&=¥frac{s-m^2}{2¥sqrt{s}}, & E&=¥sqrt{P^2+m^2},¥¥
R&=p_{¥mathrm{Out}}, & E'&=¥sqrt{R^2+m^2},¥¥
K&=¥sqrt{R^2+w^2+2Rw¥sin¥varphi}, &
¥sqrt{E'^2-m^2}&=R.
¥end{align*}
Energy conservation fixes $R$ through $E'+K+w=¥sqrt{s}$. The external momenta are
¥begin{align*}
k^¥mu&=P(1,-¥cos¥alpha,-¥sin¥alpha,0),¥¥
p^¥mu&=(E,P¥cos¥alpha,P¥sin¥alpha,0),¥¥
k'^¥mu&=(K,-w¥cos¥varphi,-R-w¥sin¥varphi,0),¥¥
p'^¥mu&=(E',0,R,0),¥¥
q'^¥mu&=w(1,¥cos¥varphi,¥sin¥varphi,0).
¥end{align*}
The momentum products that contain every propagator denominator are
¥begin{align*}
D_+&¥equiv2k'¥!¥cdot q'=2w(K+w+R¥sin¥varphi),¥¥
D_-&¥equiv-2k¥!¥cdot q'=-2Pw¥,[1+¥cos(¥alpha-¥varphi)],¥¥
t&=(p'-p)^2=2m^2-2EE'+2PR¥sin¥alpha.
¥end{align*}
To shorten phases without hiding kinematics, define only
¥[z_a=e^{i¥alpha},¥quad ¥bar z_a=e^{-i¥alpha},¥qquad z_f=e^{i¥varphi},¥quad ¥bar z_f=e^{-i¥varphi}.¥]
